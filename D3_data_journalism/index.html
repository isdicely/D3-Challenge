Segment 3: Obesity and Age


We continue to decode information from the U.S. Census Bureau and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System. This installment looks at obesity and age across America. We show the median age by state and it's percent obesity in 2014.




Insights on Obesity and Age Across America (2014)

  • Healthiest States (lower obesity percentages):
    • Distric of Columbia (DC)
    • Colorado (CO)
    • Hawaii (HI)
  • Youngest State Population (lower median age):
    • Utah (UT)
    • Arkansas (AK)
    • Distric of Colombia (DC)

There appears to be a correlation between age and obesity. As median age of the population in a state increased the obesity percent of the population appears to increase.
